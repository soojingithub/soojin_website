Context
‍

Ringle is a fast-growing online English learning platform based in South Korea. Ringle provides two services: a paid tutoring service, and a free content service. Among 100K+ signed-in users, only 15K of the users are on a paid tutoring service ($37/hour). There exists a barrier to entry.

Challenge
‍

Improve on Ringle's existing content service to provide an affordable content subscription service that can be implemented within 6 months.

Problem
‍
I have identified some pain points of using Ringle's current content service.

Reflection

Empathize with Users & Ask Why

The problem was not at the quality of the service but rather at the accessibility and the usability of the service. Through an extensive user interview, I was able to identify users' core needs.

Accessible and Affordable
‍
I am hopeful that the subscription-based content service will allow more users to access learn English with Ringle at more affordable price.
